# furniture-profitability-analysis
Business Intelligence project analyzing retail profitability, pricing, and operations using SAS Viya

**Furniture Retail Profitability & Operations Analysis
**Overview
Analyzed 2,121 retail transactions to identify key drivers of profitability, optimize pricing strategies, and improve operational decision-making.

****Business Problem
Retailers were experiencing inconsistent profitability despite strong sales volume. This project identifies inefficiencies in discounting, product mix, and regional performance.

****Tools Used
SAS Viya
Excel

****Key Insights
High discounts (40–70%) significantly reduce profitability
Chairs and Furnishings drive profit, while Tables generate losses
High-volume regions do not always produce high profit
Consumer segment shows high revenue but volatile margins

****Recommendations
Reduce investment in low-performing products (Tables)
Implement discount caps (~30–40%)
Customize regional strategies for underperforming areas
Use forecasting to anticipate demand

Impact
This analysis demonstrates how data can improve pricing strategy, inventory planning, and operational efficiency.
